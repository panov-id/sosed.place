# Community Guidelines — sosed.place

## General principle

Content must stay within the norms of a calm, peaceful society. We moderate automatically, before publication — not after.

## Fully prohibited

Harassment, drug-related content, sex-work solicitation. Such messages are never published or sent, in the feed or in chat.

## Content with limited visibility

- **Sexual subtext.** Hidden from everyone by default. You can turn on showing it yourself in settings, by accepting a separate agreement.
- **LGBT-related topics.** On sosed.place, such messages are filtered out of the feed entirely and shown to no one.

## Reports and blocks

If a message breaks the rules, report it. Reports and blocks reduce the author's posting quota. Blocking hides a message only for you, not for everyone.

## Consequences of violations

Repeated violations can lead to a reduced quota or losing access entirely.
